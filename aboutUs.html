ABOUT US

Our Foundation

Telangana Training and Placement Officers Consortium was registered in the year 2023 vide registration number 392 of 2023 under Telangana Societies Registration Act, 2001 with the aim of creating a unified platform for effective collaboration between industries and educational institutions across the state.

Our consortium works to bridge the gap between academia and industry, ensuring that students from our member institutions are well-prepared for the demands of the professional world and have equitable access to career opportunities.

Aims & Objectives

The key objectives of Telangana Training & Placement Officers Consortium (TTPOC) are as follows:

  • → Establishing a unified platform for effective collaboration between industries and institutes.
  • → Enhancing and reconfiguring the Training and Placement Processes to ensure optimal outcomes.
  • → Facilitating increased employment prospects for students.
  • → Addressing shared concerns with solutions and issues to member TPOs.
  • → Promoting networking opportunities and best practices among engineering institutes.
  • → Ensuring equitable access to job opportunities, Memorandums of Understanding (MoUs), projects, and internships for all students of member colleges.
