Membership

There will be three types of membership

  • Membership Fee : Rs. 1,000/- per annum

  • Life Membership : Rs. 10,000/-

  • Institutional Member : Rs. 10,000/-

  • Non-Telangana Institutional/Individual Membership : Rs. 1000/-

The membership will be effective from 1st January, The General Body shall fix the membership fees from time to time.

An Institutional member can depute one representative to attend any activity such as workshop, panel discussion, conference/symposia/FDP/CDP conducted by the consortium free of cost i.e. registration fee will be waived.

Eligibility

Any Training & Placement Officer of an academic Institution within the geographical limits of state of Andhra Pradesh in India or a person working in the capacity of Director or Head or as Professor in-Charge of a functional unit in any academic institution.
