TS CODE CHAMPS COMPETITION

(A STATE LEVEL CODING COMPETION INITIATED AND ORGANISED BY TS TO CONSORTIUM)

TS TPO Consortium in collaboration with Campus Corporate Connect (CC) is planning to organize a State Level Coding competition to trigger the coding skills of engineering graduates. First level of this completion will be at district level in all 13 districts of Sunrise State Andra Pradesh. There will be no participation/registration fee. In each district one college will host the event and from every college maximum 5 teams (team size 2) will participate in it. Top 3 teams of each district will be sent to state level finals. The final event will be held in any college which is nearer to capital city Amaravathi. The top 3 teams shall be rewarded in state level competition with redeemable vouchers worth Rs. 50000/- (1st prize), Rs. 30000/- (2nd prize) and Rs. 20000/- (3rd Prize) which will be sponsored by few startups.

This competition is targeted to 2020 graduating batch students to enlighten them on current industry needs and to showcase the talent of our students to corporates. All the participants will get a participation certificate from AP TPO Consortium.
