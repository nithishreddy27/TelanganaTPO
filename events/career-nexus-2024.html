Career Nexus 2024

Bridging Talent with Opportunity

Event Details

26 & 27 April, 2024
Balavikas CSRB
9:00 AM onwards

Description

TTPOC successfully hosted Career Nexus 2024, uniting 150+ Training & Placement Officers, 20+ global tech leaders, 50+ EdTech professionals, and 100+ HR leaders. The event featured insightful panels on emerging tech trends, hiring strategies, and the evolving role of education, equipping attendees with valuable industry insights.

Beyond discussions, Career Nexus 2024 fostered meaningful networking, strengthening academia-industry collaboration. This milestone event reinforced the bridge between talent and opportunity, and TTPOC extends its gratitude to all participants for making it a resounding success.
