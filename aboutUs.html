ABOUT US

Telangana Training and Placement Officers Consortium registered in the year 2023 vide registration number 392 of 2023 under Telangana Societies Registration Act, 2001 with the following Aims and Objectives.

AIMS AND OBJECTIVES

The key objectives of Telangana Training & Placement Officers Consortium (TTPOC) are as follows:

  • Establishing a unified platform for effective collaboration between industries and institutes.
  • Enhancing and reconfiguring the Training and Placement Processes to ensure optimal outcomes.
  • Facilitating increased employment prospects for students.
  • Addressing shared concerns with solutions and issues to member TPOs.
  • Promoting networking opportunities * and best practices* among engineering institutes.
