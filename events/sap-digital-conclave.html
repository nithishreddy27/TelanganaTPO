SAP Digital Conclave

Launch of SAP Career Development Centre

Event Details

October 4, 2024
Vivanta by Taj, Begumpet
9:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Description

The SAP Digital Conclave marked a significant milestone with the launch of the SAP Career Development Centre, an initiative designed to empower students with industry-relevant skills, training, and career opportunities in the SAP ecosystem.

The event featured keynote sessions, expert panel discussions, and interactive workshops, offering insights into the latest SAP technologies, career pathways, and collaborative opportunities for students and institutions.
